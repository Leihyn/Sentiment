The Problem

Traditional AMMs use fixed fees that are suboptimal in all market conditions.

Bull Markets (Greed)

  • Traders willingly pay more
  • Fixed 0.3% fees leave money on the table
  • LPs miss potential revenue

Bear Markets (Fear)

  • Fixed fees kill trading volume
  • Traders avoid the exchange
  • LPs earn nothing

Our Solution

Counter-cyclical dynamic fees based on real-time market sentiment

Maximizes fee x volume across all market cycles

Architecture

Off-Chain: Keeper Bot

  • Aggregates 8 free data sources
  • Weighted sentiment scoring
  • Updates every 4 hours
  • $0/month data costs

On-Chain: Hook Contract

  • Uniswap v4 beforeSwap hook
  • EMA smoothing (anti-manipulation)
  • Staleness protection (6hr fallback)
  • Gas-efficient design
fee = MIN_FEE + (sentimentScore x FEE_RANGE / 100)

Security & Anti-Manipulation

EMA Smoothing

30% new data / 70% historical prevents sudden fee manipulation

newEMA = (raw x 0.3) + (current x 0.7)

Anti-Frontrunning

Randomized timing jitter (+/- 30 min) makes update times unpredictable

  • Staleness protection: Auto fallback to 0.30% after 6 hours
  • Multi-keeper support: Multiple authorized update addresses
  • Input validation: Sentiment constrained to 0-100 range
